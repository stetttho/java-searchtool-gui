 Dr. Steffen, a licensed clinical psychologist, is the author of this in-depth yet easy-to-understand book. Dr. Steffen begins by defining shame and identifying some of its sources. She discusses the effects of feelings  of shame on different aspects of one's life. Finally, she offers some  introspective exercises to help people work through and overcome shame to  find peace and self-forgiveness. A good book to help people self-analyze  their emotions.
