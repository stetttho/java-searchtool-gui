 I really love female vocals and being a Christian, I am very particular  about what I will listen to. I was so surprised and delighted to discover  not only the exceptional vocals, but also to find such sensitivity and  maturity in the lyrics. So many times it seems as if bands record albums  with the sole intention of trashing the deeply held beliefs of others.  October Project has managed to present songs about the rawness of life and  the struggle that goes along with it, while honestly searching for answers  and meaning.
