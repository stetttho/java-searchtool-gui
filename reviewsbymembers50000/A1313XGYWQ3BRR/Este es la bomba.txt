 This is one of the most outstanding albums in the history of Latin music.  Of course it contains the huge hits like "La Copa de la Vida",  "La Bomba" and "Por Arriba, Por Abajo", but don't skip  over hidden jems like "Gracias por Pensar en Mi" "No Importa  La Distancia", "Asi Es La Vida" and "Marcia  Baila". There is no wonder this CD was such a huge success and one  listen will show you why Ricky has taken the world by storm. Si hablas  espanol, seguro que ya sabes las razones por las cuales este disco es el  mejor.
