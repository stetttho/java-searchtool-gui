 This novel is a personal testimony of how Ms. Smith found direction in her life and career, thought a self development process.  The book explains how to: Use a simple, proven system to succeed in your career goals; Regain  self-esteem and faith to achieve your dreams; and Take advantage of  valuable tools to help you discover your talents  With a unique story of  her personal struggle to find purpose and identity in her career, she  inspires audiences to fulfill their dreams, develop their potential and  meet the challenges of their lives through a systematic approach to self  actualization.
