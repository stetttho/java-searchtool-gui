 Jessica's newest proves to be much better than her first album for many reasons: the songs contain more r&b and rock beats to them, her voice sounds much stronger and definitely more mature, her new ballads are more powerful and beautiful than ever. Jessica has proved on this album that she is more than just a pretty face, because God knows this girl has amazing vocal chords. Her voice really will blow you away on her ballad w/Marc Anthony called Where You Are, or on more umtempo songs like Hot Like Fire. Jessica's album has not just pop music, but pop music mixed together with r&b, rock, hip hop, and gospel. This album has something for everyone. Jessica has moved away from her bubblegum pop music and this album proves that she has definitely matured.
