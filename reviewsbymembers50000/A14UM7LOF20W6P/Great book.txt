 The major strength of this book is the way the author uses multiple characters' first-person narrations to paint the picture of the multi-dimensional characters and setting.  The book is an excellent illustration of the saying that we are all the main characters of our own dramas, and see things from our own perspectives, justifying our own beliefs and actions. Aside from that, the struggle of those close to Benjie to keep him out of the drug culture is a compelling story.
