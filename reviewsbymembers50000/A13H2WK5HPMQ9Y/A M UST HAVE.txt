 This is a great book and a great gift to give anyone who has an imagination, passion for adventure, and a thirst for mystery!!  These books are filled with beautiful, exotic, textured images of far away places.  The story is composed of letters exchanged between two people.  So when you're reading them, it's as though you are reading fantastical letter that you've come upon one day.  As you're reading these letters, you're pulled into this mystical realm of Griffin & Sabine!!
