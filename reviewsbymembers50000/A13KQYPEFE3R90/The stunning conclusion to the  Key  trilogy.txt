 It's Zoe's turn to find her key. In the midst of her quest, Kane's attacks grow more desperate as he sees his own victory disappearing. Love grows between Zoe and Bradley, making her believe maybe she'll find what she never thought she'd have. dangers beckons ever more as she grows closer tot he end of her quest.  There was more about the legend of the Sisters at the end of this story, but not enough to give real fulfillment to the readers. It would have been nice to see more of what happens after the Sisters of Glass are freed and the land of the Gods rejoices.
