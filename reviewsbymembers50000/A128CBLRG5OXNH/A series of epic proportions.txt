 This is the season where Babylon 5 turned from simple space opera to a series of epic proportions. There are climatic episodes where the characters of Babylon 5 must take decisive action in order to uphold the principles for which they hold dear. Others sink into a dark quagmire by embracing the forces of evil and doom, for which they will pay in later years. Season 3 is a must have for Babylon 5 fans as well as anyone with a passing interest into what this show is all about.
