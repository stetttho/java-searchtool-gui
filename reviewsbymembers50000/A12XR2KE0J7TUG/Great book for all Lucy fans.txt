 This book is a must-have for all fans of Lucille Ball and I Love Lucy. Entertaining, well-researched and authoritative, this book tells you everything you could ever want to know about the production and history of I love Lucy. The episode guide which lists plots, guest stars, songs, etc - is indispensable. There are tons of great photos too. This book is absolutely wonderful - makes  you want to turn on the TV right away and watch a classic episode.
