 Corbet serves up a light contemporary romance that focuses on the difference between guys and girls, only to discover they have common insecurities.  Mia Foley, a quiet and pretty violist, begins falling for tennis player Will Holland just as he is starting to fall for her.  Narrative volleys from Will to Mia as they move from superficial stereotypes to distinct personalities.  There humor plus romance style may appeal to fans of Sonya Sones and Ron Koertge, although the writing and themes are not as mature.
