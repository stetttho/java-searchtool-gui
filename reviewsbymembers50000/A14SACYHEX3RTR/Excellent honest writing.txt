 Smack is a gritty novel about the agonies and ecstacies of herion addition.  Two British teens run away because they feel cramped by their parents and live a life on the streets.  Becoming squatters, they must  resort to a life of crime to support their new habit.  A tragic and  realistic story, told in many different voices.  Pair with the White Horse  by Cynthia D. Grant and Beauty Queen Linda Glovach by for a great book  discussion.
