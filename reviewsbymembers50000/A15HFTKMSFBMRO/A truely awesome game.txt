 This game takes place during china's 3 kingdom period. This game has 2 player modes that let 2 people play on the same team in a level. The 2 players can also face each other in Vs mode. The Vs mode has multiple levels to choose from each with a different goal. The game also adds elephant troops. There are over 40 people that are available to play as. The graphics are not bad either. I won't say any more just go rent the game it speaks for itself
