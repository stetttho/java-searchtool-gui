 This is The Rasmus' second international single.  The single contains four tracks. The tracks are: 1. Guilty (the North American and UK mix)  2. Play Dead (Bjork cover)  3. Used To Feel Before (song released on "Into" Special Edition)  4. Guilty Video.  For any one else who is a fan of The Rasmus, this is a must have.  To any one else who may be knew to The Rasmus, this single is also a good buy because the bonus tracks give you more of their work to sample.  If you only own The Rasmus' newest album "Dead Letters" and want more from The Rasmus,  check out The "Funeral Song" single or the Special Edition release of "Into".
