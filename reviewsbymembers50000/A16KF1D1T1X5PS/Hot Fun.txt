 This is a very sexy and offbeat little movie, featuring a very supple and provocative young lady who is blessedly topless on many occasion in the film. It's not what you would describe as an Edge Of Your Seat Thriller, so watch it as the first half of a double feature, or better yet, go straight to the bedroom activities as soon as the credits roll.   What my wife and I liked most about this movie was the erotic nudity, and the unpredictability of the story. Definitely not a typical Hollywood movie.
