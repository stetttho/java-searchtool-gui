 These are the 1940's music videos, of the musical greats, such as: Count Basie, Nat'king'Cole, Duke Ellington, Louis Armstrong, Billie Holiday, Dizzy Gillespie, The Delta Rhythm Boys, Billy Eckstine, The Mills Brothers, Cab Calloway, Fats Waller, and Louis Jordan & The Tympani 5; just to name a few. All filmed live at the Cotton Club,the Apollo theater; and the Savoy Ballroom in the Golden age of harlem, N.Y. A MUST SEE.
