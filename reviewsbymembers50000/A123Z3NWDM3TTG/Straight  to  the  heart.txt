 These guys  are  the  bomb. This whole CD is very good. If you like to groove to smooth sounds of R&B jazz, this is the CD for you. As you listen to this CD, it will take you away to different jungle type sounds and melodies. smooth with a touch of rhythm. The vocal song "Heartfelt," is a smooth and lovely love song. This CD is great for a quiet dinner for two. I highly recommend this one.
