 Old-timers waiting for another Kansas "classic" to turn up-don't hold your breath! You won't find it with "Somewhere to Elsewhere". Gone are the melodic movements and brilliant arrangements  as demonstrated on "Song for America" and "Masque".   Instead Kansas, puts forth a "tired" sounding effort, void of the  old intense musicianship. Kerry Livgren, the creative force behind Kansas  needs to channel his energies "elsewhere". Forget this one, and  remember Kansas in their glory- circa 1974-75......
