 This a a quiet, thoughtful piece of film making. It takes effort and braincells but once you get into it, the result is well worth it! Fanny is quietly strong among her amoral cousins and licentious neighbors. She is  not a Hollywood glamour girl because that was not her personality. Fanny is  a strong moral figure and an excellant role model for girls. The good girl  gets the guy for once! Just relax and watch a complex tale unfold before  your eyes!
