 The next stop in the U-Turn series, "More God Allows U-Turns - True Stories of Hope and Healing", gives the reader another dose of the u-turn doctrine: No matter how far you've traveled the wrong road in life, regardless the wrong choices you have made, and the terrible situations you've found yourself, God Allows U-Turns and He gives you the grace to get back on track. An inspirational book that breathes life into the hope of second, third and fourth chances, "More God Allows U-Turns" is a must-read for 2002.
