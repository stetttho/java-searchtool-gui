 This compilation is the ultimate collection of a legendary salsa artist and one of the original Fania All Stars. For those who are all time fans of New York Salsa and followed the careers of some greats like Willie Colon, Hector Lavoe and the late Celia Cruz will definitely recordar with this fine nostalgic collection of salsa hits. Not too much to say other than it is superb and for sure you will find the majority of your original favorites on this album. The really big hits of all time are: El Gran Varon, Pirana, Calle Luna, Calle Sol and La Murga. A must have if you like classic "old school" salsa.
