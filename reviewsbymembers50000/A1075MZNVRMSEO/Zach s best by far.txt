 Zacarias Ferreira always manages to stay among the top five bachateros and this album is a great of example of why he is popular. By far his best bachata this album has a "country-western" flare to it. Although it is true bachata music you will definitely hear nuances of other genres. If you are avid follower of this rhythm for sure you will recognize the first two songs "Te amo" and "Chica de mi barrio". Those two songs topped the charts however, don't hesitate to let this whole album play right through to the end. You are in for a pleasant surprise.
