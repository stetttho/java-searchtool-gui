 This album Juntos con sus exitos is excellent especially if you want to have some their first merengue hits. The cd offers songs from the past, which bring nice memories of the early years of this successful Dominican group. There are several good songs in this cd such as Borron y cuenta nueva, Mi tonto amor, Ya viene el Lunes y Brindame una copa just to mention a small numbers of great tracks. As you already know, if you are a fan of Los Hermanos Rosario that they have 25 year successful music history and if you buy any music from the past it will definitely include some of their greatest hits. This cd in particular has most of their renowned songs.  Enjoy this cd you deserve it!
