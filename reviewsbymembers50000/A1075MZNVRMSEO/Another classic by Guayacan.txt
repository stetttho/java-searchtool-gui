 With this album Guayacan treats us to another classic album that is full of great salsas. To tell you the truth the whole cd is wonderful with both fast and slow songs. There is definitely a varied style. The rhythm is typical of Guayacan, therefore if you know their style, you surely will identify with what I'm saying. In a few words, the cd is excellent and is a musical treasure for any salsa lover. This is some great music for dancing. Do enjoy!
