 This is album is a wonderful tribute to the late Hector Lavoe who was one the greats of the unforgettable 70's era. Junior Gonzalez exemplified to perfection some the infamous tunes that followers of Lavoe treasure. Tunes that brought 70's salsa to a level only matched by artists of calibre like Lavoe and other Fania all stars. Surely enough you have music from Lavoe in your repertoire but this tribute is a worthy souvenir that you definitely could add to your collection of Hector Lavoe's incomparable salsa music. Do enjoy!
