 What a wonderful cd this is by "El Gringo de la bachata". This bachata singer who is not as recognized as some of his counterparts is definitely talented and will be one of the more renowned bachateros in short period of time. This particular cd speaks for itself. The cd includes very romantic tunes that really exemplify the beauty of bachata music. All are romantic bachatas with very profund lyrics and catchy rhythms. If you are looking for a truly romantic bachata cd from start to finish I recommend this one. All the songs are excellent. A todos los seguidores de la buena bachata este disco es para ustedes. I highly recommend this unique cd by "El Gringo" .
