 Anyone who has seen Joss Stone live and therefore heard her exceptional voice, will acknowledge that she's one of the greatest new stars on the music scene since Norah Jones. Joss' voice reminds us of Janis Joplin, but I would say that Joss' voice is more refined, especially at her age. Her debut CD "The Soul Sessions" is an absolute "must" for every music collection. The phenomenal thing is the tremendous range of her voice, the volume and the professionalism Joss' uses to exercise her rare gift.
