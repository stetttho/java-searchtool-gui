 This 87 minute CD covers 650 hot topics in audio format in the same manner as the author's "Hot Topics: Flashcards for Passing the PMP Exam".  It's a painless way to learn the key terms and their official definitions, which you will encounter on the PMP exam.  If used in conjunction with the paper flashcards you'll have a powerful two-pronged approach to preparing for the exam.  The terms and definitions are up-to-date and reflect the content of the PMBOK 2000 edition.
