 The 1st book in the Harry Potter Series is a must for all readers of any age.   Harry Potter was orphaned at a very young age and left in the care of his horrible aunt and uncle. He leads a very dismal life until one day a giant named Hagrid visits and announces that he is a wizard and gives him an acceptance letter to the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft sending him sprawling on a thrilling journey of self acceptance and friendship.   You will be very glad to get a chance to barge into a world of wizards, goblins, centaurs, and unicorns while Harry makes many friends as well as enemies, and learns the truth about his parents death and the destiny that awaits him.
