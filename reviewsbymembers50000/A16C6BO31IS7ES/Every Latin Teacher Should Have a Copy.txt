 This book should be in every Latin teacher's reference library.  Included are 24 outstanding, practical and thought-provoking articles written by experienced and knowledgable Latin teachers and professors, from the elementary to collegiate level. Among the many topics treated in this book are: teaching methodologies, curriculum guidelines, classroom enrichment, reading fluency, learning disabilities, distance education, linguistics and the standards movement. There are articles aimed at elementary school, middle school, high school and university level Latin teachers and professors. For those who want to go deeper into the topics explored, there is an excellent list of works cited. This is the first comprehensive book about Latin teaching published in over a quarter of a century. You will find yourself referring to it over and over again. I do!
