 If you enjoyed The Nightmare movies, then this is the collection for you. All the blood, gore, and wickedness that kept you awake at night after watching one of these is all here including 2 pairs of 3D glasses for Nightmare 3, a little book that tells about each movie, and and 8th DVD that shows never before seen scenes on all 7 movies. A must for Nightmare fans and a good choice for any horror fan.
