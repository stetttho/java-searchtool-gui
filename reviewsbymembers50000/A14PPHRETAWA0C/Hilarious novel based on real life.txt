 This comic read is truly based on real life.  The reader meets Bridget a single, young English woman.  She is constantly being pestered by her mother and all of her mother's friends about being single.  She is ready to start off a new year by making herself new year's resolutions such as: to quit smoking and to lose weight.  Helen Fielding wrote a truly brilliant novel.  The characters are easy to relate to and will make the reader laugh out loud.  Read this for a peek inside a woman's soul.
