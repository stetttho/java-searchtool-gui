 I love James and the Giant Peach, and still do at 35. The shame, though, are the illustrations, which are nothing like the dreamy, haunting pictures of Nancy E. Burkert. This is why I give 3 stars only. (...)  (...)  If you're lucky enough to get your hands on a used copy of the first edition of this book, you'll immediately see the difference and wonder why this edition was not graced,as it had been before, with Burkert's incomparable art.
