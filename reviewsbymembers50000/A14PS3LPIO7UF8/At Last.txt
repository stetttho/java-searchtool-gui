 At last; Hendrix' final project was finished in a way that resembled his own vision. His later music was clearly of a spiritual nature; which in restrospect, was inevitable. His earlier work more than hinted at it; as did the man's essential nature.  These songs are not new, although they remain "new" every time one listens to them. But apart from the well spoken of acomplishments Hendrix made by the time these were recorded, they remain an intregal part of our lives. These were the songs that helped define and lay the foundation for a uniquely American spirituality through musical experience.
