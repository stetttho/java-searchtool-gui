 Sarah McLachlan is an amazing artist who has lots of talent. This album in particular has some of the best tracks from her. The most famous songs of McLachlan are here: Building a Mystery, Sweet Surrender, Adia (my favourite) and Angel. She will really take your breath away. You can listen to this album over and over again and it will always be in your collection. Surfacing is the album to listen to if you are not familiar with Sarah McLachlan and the bottom line is, this album is amazing.
