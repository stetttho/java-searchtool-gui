 My God, if you love prog rock, get this cd.  If you love beautiful harmonies and great lead voice, get this cd.  If you love the perfect blend of acoustic and electric, get this cd.  In fact, if you simply want a most magnificent disc, by Stupid Dream! Imagine the best of Pink Floyd, (old) Genesis, ELP, and combine that will some contemporary prog sound, and here is this disc.  It is fabulous! You know how, especially with prog rock, it takes a while to get used to?  Well, with this disc, I fell in love with it upon first listening!!!
