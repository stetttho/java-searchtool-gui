 This book is ecellent.  It is formatted in the style of questions and answers.  The answers are always illuminating and the topics covered range through some of the following - Discovering the inner Guru and listening to its guidance - Learning about Shaktipat (spiritual power awakening within) - Questions about the inner and outer guru - Expositions on different Mantras and a whole range of other topics that are all useful to the spiritual seeker.  The answers given are often concise and given with wamth and love and this is what shines through in these pages.  If you are a beginner on the spiritual path or even if you have been walking it for years, you should add this book to your collection.  It's a definite keeper and will provide you with much insight along your journey.
