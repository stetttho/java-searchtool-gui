 This book is layed out in the easy to read style of a few pages per chapter and in a question and answer style.  The questions asked of Nisargadatta Maharaj are on a broad range of subjects.  The answers given are always thoughtful and profound.  So profound in fact that you have to stop reading and ponder them as you go.  Topics addressed iclude: - Suffering - Pleasure and pain - Desire - The false and the real  to name but a few.....  This book will lead you towards a union with the absolute through the stripping away of everything you think you are and know.
