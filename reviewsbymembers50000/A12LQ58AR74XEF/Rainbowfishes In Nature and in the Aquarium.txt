 Marvelous book with color photos and technical descriptions of each species including general features, aquarium care, breeding, distribution & natural habitat, and status. Includes numerous personal suggestions for care and rearing. Includes separate sections on rainbowfishes of Australia, rainbowfishes of New Guinea and blue-eyes of Australia & New Guinea. List of correction/errata can be found at: http://www.rainbowfishes.org/AllenErrata.html
