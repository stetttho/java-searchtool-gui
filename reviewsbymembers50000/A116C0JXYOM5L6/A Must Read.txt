 Anyone interested in understanding the social fabric of the United States must read this book.  It helps one to understand how a large portion of the African American community could embrace such a man.  It leaves you reeling  as it reveals some of the dogma of the Nation of Islam and information  about the Pakistani known as Wallace Dodd Fard, the true founder and  mastermind behind the exploitation of the disenfranchised.  Chilling.   Brace yourself.
