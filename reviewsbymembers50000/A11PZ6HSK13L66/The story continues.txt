 Miaka has returned home, but she finds that she isn't able to pull her mind away from the world she left behind. Her thoughts drift to her friends and to Tamahome, despite her family's encouragement to focus on her exams.   Eventually, Miaka comes to realize that Yui has been pulled into the book and Miaka journeys back to the book world, only to discover heartbreaking truths about Yui and what her role as Priestess will entail.
