 In this autobiography, Grace Slick formerly of Jefferson Airplane, gives an full account of her life in the rock and roll spotlite. She gives up the goods on lots of interesting and famous personalities in one of the most exciting times in rock and roll history.   Although sometimes a bit irritatingly egocentric, Ms. Slick has so many interesting tidbits to disclose that it's easy to forgive her overabundance of self-love.   An interesting, entertaining and often funny book, just wish it had focused more on the 60's.
