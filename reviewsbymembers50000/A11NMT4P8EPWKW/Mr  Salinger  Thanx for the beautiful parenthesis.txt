 Never have I read a more self-indulgent author and never have I been more entertained! Seymour told Buddy, "I mind getting a rattling good story from you. I want your loot." J.D. Salinger gives you his loot in both of these stories. I finished "Seymour: An Introduction" much the same way I exited my first Roller Coaster ride - scared, breathless and eager to go back again.
