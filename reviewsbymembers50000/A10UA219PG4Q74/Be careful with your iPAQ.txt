 Nearly every Pocket PC website recommends that you do not use adhesive-style screen protectors with the iPAQ due to how its screen is constructed. You risk permanently damaging your iPAQ screens flexible outer layer (the touch sensor part) when you remove the adhesive protector.  It's not clear from this product's description, either here or on the Companionlink website whether or not there is an adhesive on this product. You may be better off using homemade protectors (out of transparencies) than an adhesive protector.
