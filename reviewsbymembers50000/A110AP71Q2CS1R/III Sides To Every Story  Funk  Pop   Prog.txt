 Great album. The first third of the album is funk metal, the second third is acoustic pop-rock and the latter third is progressive rock.  And it's all brilliant -- sometimes tragic, often times sad, this concept album  has many facets, many faces.  But it's the finale, "Who Cares?",  that is the highlight (on an album that is full of highlights).  Queen has  "Bohemian Rhapsody", Extreme has "Who Cares?", the most  moving and powerful song I've ever heard. 'Nuff said.
