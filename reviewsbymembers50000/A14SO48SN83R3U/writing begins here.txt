 An excellent  foundational writing book for those who want to begin writing but have little practice or knowledge on how to start.  Although it offers very helpful strategies for beginning to write, this book's basic rule is that there are no rules for writing...just do it!  Her  own highly personable style of writing makes Natalie Goldberg your personal writing coach and motivating guide.  She will have you 'writing down your bones' before you even finish reading her book.
