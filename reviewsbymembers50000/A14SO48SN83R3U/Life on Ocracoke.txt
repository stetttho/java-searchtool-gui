 If you have ever been charmed by the island of Ocracoke, North Carolina and wondered what it might be like to live there, this book is for you. The book contains accounts from a number of Ocracoke dwellers, some of whom are native to the island and some who've transplanted themselves there from other areas. The accounts detail what the residents like and don't like about Ocracoke living, the reasons some have transplanted themselves there and their predictions and hopes about the island's future in this age of soaring real estate prices and commercial over development. The book provides a very interesting look at the Ocracoke island life from those who have lived it.
