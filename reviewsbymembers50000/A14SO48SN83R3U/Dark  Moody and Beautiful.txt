 On Bitter, Ndegeocello takes her listeners on a cathartic musical journey into the raw, emotional depths of love's bittersweetness.    Ndegeocello sets her articulate and passionate lyrics to slow, haunting melodies and sullen grooves.  The result is a soulfully melancholic but beautiful blend of folk, rock and mellow jazz elements. On a number of selections, Ndegeocello's musical poetry just flows so effortlessly from a hypnotic mix of strings and acoustic guitar. They will carry you away.  Bitter is for listeners who can appreciate a versatile artist whose music defies rigid categorization and for those whose palate can tolerate the sometimes bittersweet taste of love.
