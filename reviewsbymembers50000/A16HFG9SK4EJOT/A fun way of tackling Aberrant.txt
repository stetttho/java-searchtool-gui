 Much as they did for Trinity, White Wolf has begun putting out mini-books touching on specific aspects of Aberrant.  These books offer a quick overview of their topic and are designed to help a GM explore a specific idea. With the rise of Aberrants, it didn't take long for every religious group to have an opinion on the superhuman creatures.  The Church focuses in on the religious side of things...  from Aberrant worship to modern day witch hunts.
