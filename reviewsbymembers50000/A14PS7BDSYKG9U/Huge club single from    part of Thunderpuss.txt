 This single comes from 1 half of Thunderpuss and it is a good mix of over the top house and DIVA vocals. This single comes with 4 mixes that are similar to the Thunderpuss sound. The radio edit(3:42) and Chris Cox Bootleg Mix(9:18) are the main  mainstream  mixes here. The next mix is the Chris Cox Club Mix(8:09) and the Hard Cox Dub Mix(7:12) are more aggressive in mix styles. The vocals are by Sandra Edwards and she puts up a storm of vocals to match the music. This follows up the number one hit by Thunderpuss & Barnes "Head".
