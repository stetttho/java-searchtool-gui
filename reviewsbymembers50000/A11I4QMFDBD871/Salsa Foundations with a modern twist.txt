 There's too much 'cheesy' salsa on the market today - so  maintains Guistino Riccio, leader of Bio Ritmo, and BIO RITMO seeks to eliminate the fluff from salsa with its lively, almost rocking salsa beat that just doesn't quit. Bio Ritmo attempts to capture the foundations of the classic salsa sound - but with a contemporary twist. If you listen hard, you can hear the rock and soul influence underlying Bio Ritmo's remakes of classic salsa - including occasional synthesizer and classic keyboard riffs.
