 If only one Paraguayan harp recording were to be considered for a collection, Eralio Gill's ART OF THE PARAGUAYAN HARP better be right up there ahead of the competition: it's that good - and represents the standard songs and styles the Paraguayan harp is so suited for.  Gill's music choices highlight the Paraguayan harp, the national instrument of Paraguay, presenting those classic tunes with a flair, whether they be quiet classical-tinged or flamboyant string-ripping flamenco-style trills.
