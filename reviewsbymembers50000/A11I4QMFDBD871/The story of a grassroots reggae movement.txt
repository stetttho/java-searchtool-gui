 If reggae music is your passion, don't miss the lovely REGGAE ON THE RIVER 2-DVD set: it captures in depth and detail the story of Humboldt County California annual Reggae on the River festival, now into its 20th year. Disc 1 focuses on the story of how the festival evolved from a fund-raiser to replace a community center torched in an arson fire to its present incarnation as one of the largest, most popular music festivals in California.  Disc 2 of REGGAE ON THE RIVER provides a focus on the music itself, gathering the finest moments from the 3-day festival's 20th anniversary show and including bonus menus and tracks.
