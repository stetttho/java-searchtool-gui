 "The Mummy Returns" is a prime example of everything wrong with big budget Hollywood movies these days: bloated excess, no plot, no character development and absolutely no concern for the intelligence of its audience.  Sure, if your a lobotomized squirrel you may find something remotely entertaining in this waste of celluloid.  But if you have even a couple of functioning neurons chances are you'll just keep looking at your watch waiting for this thing to end. The first film was fun, this one is merely tedious.  The sad thing is, as long as we keep handing the studios are hard earned cash for drivel like this they'll just keep on churning them out.
