 This easy to read, entertaining book makes one feel it IS possible to achieve a healthy weight & physical condition.  It helps one to understand that just because one is not thin by nature doesn't mean that one CAN'T, using some simple tips & tricks, look & feel svelte & sexy.  The anecdotes, recipes, and non-judgmental philosophy (French Zen, sort of) in this book all blend together to create something appetizing & tasty that goes down easily & will stick with you long after you finish reading.
