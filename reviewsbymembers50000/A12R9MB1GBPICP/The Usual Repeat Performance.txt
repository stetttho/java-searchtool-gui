 Shakatak has done it again.  "Magic" is the latest masterpiece  from the jazz group out of the U.K., and what a CD it is.  It's rich smooth  jazz compositions are the epitome of contemporary jazz and, again, puts the  group right up there with all the other jazz greats.  The title track,  "Magic," is a rich blend of vocals and instrumentals with a beat  that will make even the most conservative people let their hair down.  If  you haven't added "Magic" to your collection, do so post haste.   It will having you movin' and groovin' whenever you play it.
