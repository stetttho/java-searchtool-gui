 Clarice Bean is the most utterly sensible person she knows, and to top it off, she has a head filled with brilliant ideas.  All of this, though, doesn't stop her from falling afoul of know-it-all teachers, dreary school assignments, strangely-behaving grandfathers, and suspiciously-disappearing best friends.  Lauren Child, the author (and creator of the new Playhouse Disney cartoon, "Charlie and Lola"), is one of the actual, not-to-be-questioned, funniest people writing books today!
