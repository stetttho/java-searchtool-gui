 When will people learn that stuffing 4 CD's full of Full Motion Video (FMV) does *not* make a video game?  X-Files:The Game is more like an interactive movie that a computer game.  This genre died with Night Trap on the Sega CD.  However, if you're an X-Files fan and enjoy a good 'whodunnit' this game is still a good buy (just try to find it on sale).
