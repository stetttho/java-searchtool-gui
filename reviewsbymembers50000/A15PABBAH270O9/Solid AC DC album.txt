 In the first decade of the new millenium, signs are that AC-DC is going to see a revival as Abba did in the last decade of the previous millenium.  For those who listened to AC-DC in their teens and want a refresher, either this or Back in Black should occupy the No. 1 slot on your nostalgia shopping list. Old school, definitely, juvenile absolutely. But your great grandkids will be listening to it for sure. Get it before John Ashcroft makes it illegal to own a copy. :-)
