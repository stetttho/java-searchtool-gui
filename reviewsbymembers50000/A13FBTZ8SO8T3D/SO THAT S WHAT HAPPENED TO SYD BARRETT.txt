 Certainly a vanity project if there ever was one, but quite listenable all the same. In the late 60's and early 70's this might have passed for a near-death experience. As it is, here in the 21st Century, it all a bit schizoid, man. But a lot of fun. Hopefully, Wilson had his tongue in cheek. Some passages soar beautifully in pre-Dark Side Pink Floyd mode. This could almost have been titled "Son of Meddle." Anyway, you'll never consider YES's TALES excessive again. Now on to DEADWING....
