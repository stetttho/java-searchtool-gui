 The private world of thirty international personalities.This book offers an insight into the private lives(and homes) of 30 very well known personalities. Not merely photographs of superstars homes,it's more like being behind the person thats meeting the stars,and viewing the rooms that make up thier lives. Of particular note and for no particular reason, here are some of the homes you'll be sneaking a peek into:Kirk Douglas,Mike Nichols,Princess Margaret,James Caan,Barbra Streisand, Ali McGraw,Malcolm Forbes,John Wayne, and many more.
