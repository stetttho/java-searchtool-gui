 WAS IT ENOUGH THAT HE HAD OBEYED ORDERS WITHOUT QUESTION?  ernst Graber wondered as he lay in the Russian mud, the greasy stink of death in his nostrils  and in his ears the roar of artillery and the whine of machine-gun bullets. And then out of the habitual sight of horror and desolation he discovered, in the passion of a brief tragic interlude, that there was love well as hate, and at last he found an answer....and a vindication.
