 With ex-Procol Harum guitarist and Hendrix acolyte Robin Trower on board as the main supplier of instrumental atmospherics, Ferry manages to completely denature R&B classics such as "Rescue Me" and "I Put a Spell On You," an intriguing, if possibly dubious, accomplishment.  The most romantic record around?  I'm not sure about that, but, hey, whatever floats your boat.
