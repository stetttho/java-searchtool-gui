 Tired of Christmas time schlock?  This is one that you can sink your teeth into -- and besides, the money goes to a good cause (International Special Olympics). Standout cuts:  John Scofield and the Wild Magnolias absolutely burning down "Go Tell it on a Mountain" and Angelique Kidjo's mountain 'o overdubs version of "O Holy Night" in which she not only sings the words but all the instrumental parts as well. Way cool.
