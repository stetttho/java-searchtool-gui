 This is a good book to keep on the kitchen counter during the Christmas holidays and anytime you are tempted to O.D. on sugar! Author S.J. Wise has written a personal, practical, user-friendly book to motivate cookie addicts to step into a happier, healthier, sugar-free lifestyle. Living in a culture where sugar addiction is wide-spread and accepted, we'd all be wise to keep this book handy as a frequent reminder that sugar is a drug, not a food.  Suza Francina, mother and author, The New Yoga for People Over 50 and Yoga and the Wisdom of Menopause
