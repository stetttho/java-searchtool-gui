 To talk about the significance of this outstanding pianist is to talk about one of the most distinguished pianists of the Century. His musicality and technique were superb. A magnificent musical Ambassador of his native Hungary and other additional evidence of the musical tradition of that beloved country who saw to born artists of the stature of Sandor Vegh, Erwin Niyreghihazi, Bela Bartok, Joseph Szigetti, Edith Farnadi, Ferenc Fricsay, Andor Foldes, Gyorgy Sandor and Andras Schiff among others. A true champion in what concerns to Liszt 's playing.  He was the first pianist who gave the European premiere of Bartok 's  Piano Third Concerto with Adrian Boult conducting.
