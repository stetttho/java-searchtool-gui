 Andras Schiff shows once more why he is on the top of the ten best pianist in the world. His impressive phrasing, his superb expressiveness and supreme panache have deserved to him the most outstanding comments all around the world. Nowadays he may be the most eloquent pianist in which Schubert concerns. His multiple conversations with Sandor Vegh, his personal efforts for several Schubert Complete Sonatas around the world, and conferences are undeniable evidences for his profound devotion for this Austrian composer.
