"Friedrich Nietzsche"   This engrossing Chinese film is part of my personal list about the top twenty outstanding movies of the Nineties. A pair of male Peking Opera will confront fifty years of civil war, foreign invasion, social movements and political distortions, forced one of them to forsake his identity.  A complex but architecturally built drama in the middle of the enigmatic reality of that nation, but above all an acknowledged tribute to art against wind and tide.
