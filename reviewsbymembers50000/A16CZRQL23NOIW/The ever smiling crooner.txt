  Bobby Vinton was the eternal baby face of the crooners after Pat Boone. His style was determined obviously as the warm side of the slender melodies, and he triumphed according those footsteps, despite the ominous presence of Elvis in the early sixties. If you consider an Italian singer such Bobby Solo you will find more than a coincidence in his respective moods. The time however has been for both splendid and generous and all those teenagers who grew up crying and singing his unforgettable songs are nowadays, happy grandmothers, but the pleasant memories will always accompany him.
