  The raise and fall of this hoodlum is shown with febrile intensity in this fast paced picture. William Wellman makes a real tour de force directorial work in his best domains. Few picture portrayed with such precision and expressiveness the underworld profile in this golden decade together with Little Caesar, Scarface, Angels with dirty face among the most remarkable titles. It's useless to talk about the prodigious acting of James Cagney who formed this breathtaking trilogy with Edward G Robinson and Paul Muni as the principal actors who gave the real presence to gangster in the screen.
