 Mr. Klein is a bitter tale about an exquisite marchand, in the middle of the war. Suddenly he's caught for burocratic mistake for other Klein from jew ascendence . This is the dramatic focus of this dark picture. Joseph Losey once more swims in his favorite theme : the cruelty and moral decline in the nazi environment. Delon makes a magnificent performance as the disturbed Mr. Klein. The ending is unforgettable! Acquire this jewel.
