 Oswaldo Pugliese incarnates the authentic and purest spirit of the Argentine tango . Pugliese conducts the orchestra with perfect domain of this difficult musical expression.  He literally suggests all the tonal fan of this gloomy universe , plowed of ancestral memories , missed loves , misfortunes , laments , broken illusions , nocturnal whimsicalness and brief affairs with the night butterflies .  To listen the tango goes far beyond a simple acoustic experience . The tango somehow is a ghost nightmares loaded with memory specters about what it could have been and it never was .   The well known female argentine film maker , Maria Luisa Bamberg told once : The tragedy of the Argentine people is that he is an European in Latin America and a Latin American in Europe . this lack of center is a fundamental ingredient to consider in this passionate musical genre .
