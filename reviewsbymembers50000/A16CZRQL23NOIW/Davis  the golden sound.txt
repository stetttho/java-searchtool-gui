 When you listen Davis playing the first bars of any random  work, you are immediatly transported to another level. That kind touch comes from the soul, the spirit and then his fingers work out according this premise. In Davis you will never listen a wrong note; he owned a internal mood that overpassed every possible adjective.   And consider the presence itself elevated automatically the artistic height of every one of his musical partners; Ron Carter, Bill Evans and so many musicians acquired a new dimension becoming true poets in the real meaning of the word; poetry comes from poyesis that in greek signifies creator.  This a superior CD and a reference album in your treasured collection. Don't miss it under no pretext.
