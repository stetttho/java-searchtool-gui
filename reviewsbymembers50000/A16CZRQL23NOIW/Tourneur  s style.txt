 The peculiar artistic trajectory is signed by powerful visual metaphors that sublimate and ignite the simple script. He always directed in allegorical style, focusing in the most complex reactions of the human psyche; even it can be said that he was born foredoomed in this sense. Just a few have been able to express with such precision and creative audacious the inner dragons and fears like he did it. He was a notable inheritor of the German expressionist school. He was the same director in that astonishing gem of the Noir genre: "Detour" and the still reminded cult movie: "I walked with a zombie" of 1943.  A man falls in love with a strange and shy woman who feels the ancient curse of the panther inside her.   Paul Schrader would revive it with an uneven remake in 1982.
