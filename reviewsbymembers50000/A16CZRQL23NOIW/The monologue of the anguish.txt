 In a dark port, in the nocturnal brumes of a half illuminated bar, a man will invite to drink a distinguished mude listener . Bit along the speech too many intersections will open and clever reflections and audacious proposals will make you devour the book in just one reading from start to finish as I did in my early twenties. This is one of the remarkable personal portraits written ever. Camus will display all his integrity, feline intelligence and astonishing ability to link all an universe of multiple reflections. Go for this text. And you will convince by yourself about the statute of this philosopher, journalist, writer. What poor can the words be when you must describe a man of the height of Albert Camus.
