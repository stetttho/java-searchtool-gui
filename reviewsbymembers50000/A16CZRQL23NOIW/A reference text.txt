 The Quality Control, more than a simple discipline must be considered as a way of thinking and living. The author makes a substantial analysis around the traditional approach, enriching it with abundant examples, related with the administrative prevision, control of mistakes, reliability of the product, detection and statistical correction of failures of the process and even laws concerned with the responsibilities of the manufacturer.  In my personal experience this book was a great allied that allowed me to obtain great instruction and conferred me a major vision around the fascinating and always expansive concept of the Quality.  Recommended for the students and teachers of Industrial Engineering, Chemical Engineering and other branches of the Engineering in general. It is important to remark additionally that the subject of Quality Control, exposed in the context of costs, is of great utility fro the engineers whose objective is minimize the risk of the producer and the consumer.       Recommended.
