 This ensamble worked out as a  perfect watch mechanism . But the presence of Szerying , Fournier and Kempff warns you about the artistic height of these notable musicians . Every one of them is gifted with a deep musical sense . And that is very important due the particular conception of one work must have been motive of enriched discussions , and not the product of one only mind as usually happens in many chamber ensambles . The final result is a very unusual set touched with the most fervent beethovenian spirit.
