 In the seventies the piano was the star instrument in the Jazz. The sax made its own in the eighties as the guitar in the nineties . In this sense Joe Sample pianism revealed such kind of freshness interpretative and original pages that made of him one of the top exponents in that moment and still today .     Voices in the rain by itself meant a personal triumph for Sample and reveals as other worthable recordings , his enormous expressive moods .     When you still listen (even this is the first time for you) the radiant joy that this piece contains .    A must in your collection.
