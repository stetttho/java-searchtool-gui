 The elusive line that separates the cowardice and the bravery is shown in this emblematic and bold picture, when Audie Murphy , a youngster decides to enlist in this project, and flees under fire to be judged over his apparent lack of courage.   Based in Lillian Ross book, this cynical gaze to the core of the human soul during War environment is one of the most celebrated  and even now acclaimed War movies ever made.   A colossal statement and  brilliant exercise of boldness in those anticipatory years of the Cold War.
