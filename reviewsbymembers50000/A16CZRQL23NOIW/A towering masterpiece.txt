 While the emotional and affective world of a Litherature University teacher is breaking in one thousand pieces, he will prepare to a young and inspired pupil who will follow his traces. His wife reall neglects him and she without any doubt decides to cheat him. He will suffer in silent voice the painful betray.  Certainly, you must be in mood to appreciate this outstanding picture, but the effort will be rewarded. A superb artwork in many senses. Redgrave once more proves he was one of the complete and solid actors of his generation.  Michael Redgrave won deservedly the Cannes International Prize as Best Actor in 1951 for this role.
