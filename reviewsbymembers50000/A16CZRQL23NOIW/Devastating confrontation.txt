 An obnoxious , sadistic and even nasty man is the successful owner of a very visited restaurant. His private life is mess, his brutal behavior with her wife will lead her to take a very special revenge when she decides to initiate a love affair with a very illustrated client of that restaurant.   A true passions game will jeopardize even more this fragile relationship, where the smart factors of this black comedy will be present along the movie. Greenaway handles the camera with tantalizing intensity, exposing deliriously the hidden demons present in our dark side.   This adultery will be the sparkling beginning of violence 's spiral that will become a true hell, sinister and anguishing that eventually will lead to a macabre ending, in a very special cite to Titus Andronicus.
