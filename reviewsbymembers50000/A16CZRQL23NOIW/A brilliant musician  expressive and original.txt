 Craig Chaquico proves his immense talents are not a simple market product. He is fully enraptured, molding and conferring in every piece he plays a special profile character and personality.   His version of the famous theme Holding back the years is without doubt, the jewel of the crown. There are other successful tracks such as Blue Universe, Lights out San Francisco, Dream catcher and Midnight swim. A winner album for enjoying, dreaming, relaxation and delighting.
