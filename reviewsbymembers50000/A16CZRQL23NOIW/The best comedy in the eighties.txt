 Gilliam built a cult movie in the great sense of the word. His imaginative script turns around the burocracy and the desperation of people who lives with invisible chains in a oppresive world that controls each detail of your private life. Pryce falls in love with a outsider girl, who eventually will let him to break the rules. Watch this movie. De Niro appears has a cameo in one of the most brilliant sequences. Gilliam handles the camera like the Gods and pays his tribute to the eternal tradition of the british comedy.
