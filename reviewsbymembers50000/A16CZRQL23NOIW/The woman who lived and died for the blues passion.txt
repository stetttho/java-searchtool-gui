 Janis Joplin is a timeless legend in the sixties rock culture . Her unmistakeable voice , her deep conviction and powerful presence became in a a real icon of that decade so special and so innovative . Cry baby , Me and Bobby Mc Gee , Move over and Summertime are my favorite themes from this unique and still amazing female singer . Your collection will be incomplete without acquiring this CD. Joplin : a woman who burned live in the blues .
