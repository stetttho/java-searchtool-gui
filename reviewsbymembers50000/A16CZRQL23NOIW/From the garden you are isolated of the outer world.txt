 The garden is sinister metaphor where Gardiner finds his particular matrix . In this garden he simply exists and his codes are so naive and plenty of ingenuity that makes us smile . But for those ironical twists of fate the life -which has much more imagination that anyone of us- will lead to this pleasant and primary man in a weird chain events that will become him in a neo Icarus.  Powerful, intriguing and bitter parable about the triviality in which we are inmersed . Read this outstanding novel and many things will appear before your eyes : and please don't forget the phrase of Morpheus to Neo in Matrix: Ignorance is bliss!
