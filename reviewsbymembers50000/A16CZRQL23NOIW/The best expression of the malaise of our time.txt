 Charles is a modern day fallen Angel and his quartet of friends ,deeply immersed in protests and committed against the pollution of an industrialized consumer society. But nevertheless is far to be satisfied, the psychoanalysis, the church, the revolution do not work out as devices and soon he will make a desperate bargain with a junkie friend   May be this film can express a late homage to the French New Wave, but I think this is a simple title. Bresson ' s stature is quite difficult to entitle.  Bresson is Bresson, unique, masterful and sublime.
