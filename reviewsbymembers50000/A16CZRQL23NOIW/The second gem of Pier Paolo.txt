 Visionary account of the multiple Jesus conflicts related with ambition, greed and above all social injustices. Shot and immersed in the same spirit that his spiritual predecessors of the Italian Neo Realism :  Roselini, De Sica and Visconti tough impregnated of a strong definite politic position. Acclaimed in Venice; it is a status film after his supreme achievement: Mama Roma.  Filmed with non profesional actors, it possesses an intelectual vigor that has resisted the acid taste of time, widely.
