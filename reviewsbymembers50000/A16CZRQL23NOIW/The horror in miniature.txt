 After being exposed to a dangerous, unknown and mysterious fog, a man will grow smaller and smaller. A terrible nightmare that would become one of the most striking B series of the genre of Science Fiction, that reached a special status -together with the Film Noir- in the golden decade of this particular category.  A brief list if I may would complete this top selection: Them, The thing, Invasion of the body snatchers, I married with a monster from the outer space, The fly and this one.  Smart script, that undoubtedly influenced in that famous sixties TV series: Land of Giants.
