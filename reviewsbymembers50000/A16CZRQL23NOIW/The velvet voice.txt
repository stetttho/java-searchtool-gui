 Nat King Cole entered in the right moment , when the presence of Frank Sinatra and Dick Haymes literally made the women to suspire far beyond the stage. His delicate tune, despite not being a potent voice, sounded reluctant and expressive. Cole somehow meant for the crooners what Sidney Pottier to the acting and Paul Robeson with his unforgettable Old man river. He influenced just a whole generation who saw in him the balance and the softness comparing with let's say the nervous and fascinating Ray.  All the later generations, of male singers will always have an immense debt to him, because somehow he was the pioneer in that difficult field
