 Arturo Benedetti Michelangeli was a sumptuous pianist . His playing was precise , and his cantabile , unique . The sound he got surrounded the atmosphere and he made floating the notes . All these virtues are fundamental when you intend to win in Debussy .  The Children's corner is the peak of this album . Michelangeli combined the impresionist mood with the childhood fantasy creating this naif atmosphere so difficult to obtain . Please acquire this CD and you 'll convince yourself about the greatness of this legendary artist .
