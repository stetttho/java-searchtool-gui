 The undeniable talent of Kenneth Branagh is carved in relief with this original and astonishing thriller with close features to Noir genre.   An investigation around a woman who has lost her memory will lead by the dark passages of the mind to unexpected results. High caliber tension, an intelligent script, a fabulous edition and a top-notch cast will conform one of the most successful entries in 1991, a film that deserves a special honor seat at the moment to review the best artistic achievements in that year.
