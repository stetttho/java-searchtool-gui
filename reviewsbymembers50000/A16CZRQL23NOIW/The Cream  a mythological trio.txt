 Imagine the ensemble : Eric Clapton guitar , Jack Bruce bass and Ginger Baker in drums. This team is in the rock music something like The New York Yankees in Major Leagues. Powerful rhytms , advanced ideas and keep in mind that every one of these super gifted musician were among the top list in every one of his instruments. Think in classical rock themes as The White Room , Badge, In the sunshine of your love , The tales of Brave Ulysses or Politician. If you're a teenager, or even not, this album is a must for you because it's a decisive turning point in the rock music . Cream was a status ensemble and still we can feel his powerful influence in certain bands. A must in your collection.
