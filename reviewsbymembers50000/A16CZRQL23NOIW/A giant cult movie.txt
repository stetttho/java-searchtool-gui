 From the other side of the Atlantic a young director named Bernardo Bertolucci accepted the challenge and won with this golden jewel. A disturbed mind man finds in the conformism a succedaneum that works out an outlet valve and so, try to forget the traumatic homosexual experience suffered in his youth. The second  fire proof will come when he is forced to prove his sympathy with the Fascism Regime murdering a former professor who lives in the exile.    The originality and strength, the admirable direction of Bertolucci, and the monumental actingof Jean Louis Trintignant, makes of this film an obligated reference for all those who are interested not only for knowing the artistic background of the late winner director of The Last Emperor and The Last Tango in Paris, but also by this fundamental issue in the European Cinematography. A must!
