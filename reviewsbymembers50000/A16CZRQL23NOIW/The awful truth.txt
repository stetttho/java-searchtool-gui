 A successful attorney in Chicago (Jessica  Lange) assumes the defense about the heinous accusations of War crimes against his Hungarian father.   In search of evidence to refuse these terrible and painful statements she will discover all that glitters is not gold.  The gradual deception around her findings will lead us to a inner climax to a true implosion of inherited blame when she inquires more and more.
