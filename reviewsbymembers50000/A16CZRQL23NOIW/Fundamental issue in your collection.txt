 From time to time a title appears and becomes a cult book . This is the case of this work of 1953 . This memorable essay makes an account of the great features of the personality and character of this wonderful people , the legitimate ancestors of the western world .  The author was a greek teacher in Glasgow University between 1920 and 1944 . And also wrote In the mountain of greece and the Greek Tragedy . Kitto will reveal to the reader many unknown clues to understand the complex world and way of thinking , acting and living of the greeks . Extraordinary.
