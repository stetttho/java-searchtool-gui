 The approach to noir genre was made by Tourneur around the feelings , the people and then the final consequences with the outer world . According to those feelings to break a rule or to cross the forbidden line is not the anticipated calculus of this action but it responds to further reasons . And somehow this is the essence of the Greek tragedy .   A gas station owner with a past will meet a desperate woman and decides to involve in troubled waters which will seal his destiny .  Scorching performance of Robert Mitchum.
