 This supreme documental awarded as the Best Documental in the last Edition of the Academy is a true landmark by its own.   Since the first shot, we will make an enigmatic journey to a gelid territory, inhabited by penguins. This is a wonderful and enriching documental which deals with their lives and times, cycles of life and reproduction with those arresting landscapes.  A captivating and mesmerizing study around the merciless nature, the implacable Darwinian laws of survival and evolution.          An unforgettable experience.
