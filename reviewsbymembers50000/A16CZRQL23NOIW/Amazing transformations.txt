 Based on Paganini `s variation 24 , Lloyd Weber intermixed a set of sharp musical vignettes with a little help from his brother Julian Lloyd to built an impeccable exercise of imagination and wounding expressiveness .  Weber made these variations as a big tree with imposing branches and literally tells the Paganini ` s life his triumphs his fame and finally his descent to hell using as furtive landscape the enigmatic Caprice 24 . .  Fascinating and arresting work of irresistible spell .
