 If you really want to get close to the essential facets of the caribbean sound and what bolero means for the latinamerican people acquire this album . First at all , Leroy Holmes was the sublime orchestra that supported many recordings and succesful albums of famous leader singers and crooners of the sixties decade such as Tito Rodriguez , Chucho Avellanet , Daniel Santos and Al Zeppy among others . In this case Holmes plays a set of unforgettable classics of the popular repertoire such Inolvidable or Llanto de luna. Holmes owned this special golden touch which allowed to be the most expressive orchestra leader in those years of the latinamerican song.  An interesting buy for you and recommended for those special moments .
