 This film is real gem . Superb and loaded with cosmic poetry . Since this movie describes the insights of the WW2 in Tuscan , the Tavianni brothers avoid to describe the physical wounds of the war .  They focus on the deep impact on the psiquis and the simple behavior of a simple group of brave people for surviving. The horro you breathe for instance in the church undergrounds , the wedding and the best achievement in the middle of field are simply outstanding . One little girl with his voice in off will make her own journey and will employ her particular justice code against the enemy told in such level of poetic and expressive intensity that when you leave the cinema hall remain mesmerized .
