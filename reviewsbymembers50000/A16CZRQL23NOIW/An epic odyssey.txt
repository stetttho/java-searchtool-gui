 The experience is the final result of a bad experience. This statement of Robert Kennedy illustrates with premonitory accuracy, the anguish moments lived not only in USA but the rest of the world in those early seventies.   Ron Howard knew recreate with all the possible realism those hard times with a superb cast a supeb stage and a very adjusted script despite certain common places.  This film despite not having won the most important prize of the Academy deserves with wide argumentation to be in the elite of the top ten best American Films in the Nineties.
