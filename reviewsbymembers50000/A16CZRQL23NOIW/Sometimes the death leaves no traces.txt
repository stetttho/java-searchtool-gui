 Charlotte Rampling as always gives a perfect and subtle portrait of heavy weight emotional depth under the masterful direction of Francis Ozon .  In this enigmatic and poignat film an apparently happy married woman . Suddenly in the inscrutable sea his husband vanishes mysteriously while she is sleeping under the warmth sun . The treatment stands far from the soap melodrama and focuses in the affective readjustment and her visible efforts to restitute a new life with a new relationship leaving behind her this personal tragedy and the past ghosts.
