 There was a vast silence in the Hall after having concluded this performance. Asajina showed once more his immense gifts as conductor respect this demanding symphony. The sense of plenitude is achieved after the glorious epic passion contained in the score.   Bruckner 's Ninth as the supreme of the Musical Repertoire Gems is permeated of mythical breath and universal passion. To achieve and even express that vision is nothing easy. Many conductors have conducted it but just a few have totally triumphed.  This an invaluable musical register that deserves be part of your treasured collection.
