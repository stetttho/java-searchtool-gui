 Hail by critics as another Big, 13 Going on 30 is updated and full of heart as well as eye candy.  Jennifer Garner is convincingly innocent and sweet as a 30 year old who is really a 13 year old magically transformed.  Seeing the evil dog-eat-dog world of the fashion magazine industry through the eyes of a sheltered 13 year old is refreshing, and the lessons learned from this experience is endearing.  Garner shines like the star she is.  At first below the radar in shows like Felicity, Garner has blossomed with this comedic turn.  Other recommendations with the common theme of innocent girls who keep their sweetness, but develops spunk:  Legally Blonde, Diary of a Discount Donna, Clueless, and Roman Holiday.
