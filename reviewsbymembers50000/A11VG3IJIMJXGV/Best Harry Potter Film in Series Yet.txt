 Magic, mystery, intrigue...it's all in this third installment of the Harry Potter film series.  Things are not what it seems at Hogswart, and the Scooby Doo gang of Harry, Hermione, and Ron are back to save the day.  There are loose ends everywhere and a crazy prisoner had escaped and is coming for Harry.  A werewolf is roaming the woods of Hogwarts, and strange dark creatures search the skies, looking for Harry and the prisoner.  But in the end, all is revealed, loose ends are tied, and Harry is safe, for the time being, thanks in a large part to Hermione.
