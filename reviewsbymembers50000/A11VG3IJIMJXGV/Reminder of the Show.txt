 If I can't watch Smallville or get a hold of a Smallville book, this CD is the next best thing to get your Smallville fix.  Songs by Two Pence None the Richer, Remy Zero, an other Smallville fixtures delivers.  This Smallville CD deserves a 5 for picking songs that adds to the storytelling of the show, rather than distracts.  Smallville is one of the best young adult shows out there that understands what's hot in music.
