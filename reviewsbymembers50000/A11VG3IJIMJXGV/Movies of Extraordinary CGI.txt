 Expand your filmmaking knowledge by watching movies with wonderful cgi effects.  In this 2 movie DVD collection, you get to view an awe-inspiring ones such as New York completely covered iced over in Day After Tomorrow or Nemo's magnificent submarine in the League of Extraordinary Gentlemen.  The acting is short of anything Oscar-winning, but is appropriate for the amount of real acting required for these films.  The stars here are the special effects/cgi.  Other recommendations:  Matrix 1 and 3, Spider-Man 1 and 2, and Hellboy.
