 For those of us who like the simple, pure sound of Nanci and her guitar, this just seems a bit loud and overdone, just like her performances on the accompanying tour with the past-it Crickets. There are one or two great  tracks, like Battlefield (that's the best one) but the one about Theresa of  Avila is awful - Nanci at her most self indulgent. She needs to watch that  streak of self-indulgence; the world is not all part of the Nanci family,  except perhaps from a distance...
