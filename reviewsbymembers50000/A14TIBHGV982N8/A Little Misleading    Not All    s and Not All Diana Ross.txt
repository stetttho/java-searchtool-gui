 "Stoned Love" was the first real hit for The Supremes after Diana Ross left the group when "Someday We'll Be Together" was released in November 1969. Jean Terrell is actually the vocalist on "Stoned Love." Also, it didn't reach number 1 = it made it to #7 on November 21, 1970. "The Boss" also never made it to the first position peaking at number 19 on August 18, 1979. "I'm Coming Out" made it to #5 on October 04, 1980.
