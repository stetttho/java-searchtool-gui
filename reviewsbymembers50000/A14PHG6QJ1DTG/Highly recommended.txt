 Securities Regulation is one of the hardest courses in law school, not least because it requires close reading of highly technical statutes. Unfortunately, there are relatively few well-done guides to securities law.  Steinberg's text is one of the best--if not the best. He combines detailed  textual review of major concepts with extensive references to both primary  and secondary sources. As such, practicing securities lawyers will also  find it a useful source of precedents.
