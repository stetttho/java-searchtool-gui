 This movie deals mainly with a 30 year veteran of the Marine Corps. who is put on trial for the accused ordering of 82 civilians in Yemen. Colonel Terry Childers ( Samuel L Jackson ) and Colonel Harry Hodges ( Tommy Lee Jones ) both served together in Vietnam and were highly decorated for their valor, but during a skirmish 28 years later in Yemen Chlders is sent to rescue the trapped Ambassador ( Ben Kingsley ) from the U.S. Embassy that is under deadly fire from civilans. During that time Childers orders his men to fire on the crowd who is armed, and in the process kills 82 supposed civilans along with women and children. Hodges, after Vietnam ends, becomes a lawyer, and when Childers is sent to trial for murder he asks Hodges to be his lawyer.
