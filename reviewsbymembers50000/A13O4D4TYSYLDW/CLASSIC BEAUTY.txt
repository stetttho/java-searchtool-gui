 Car lovers are going to have an unexpected  chance to drive all the legendary models like JAGUAR MK II or the MINI-COOPER or my favorite Ford Escort RS2000 and many many more. One of the best racing simulators in the market comes from Simbin and offers something totally new in this huge game category.  After playing for a few minutes this game you are going to be amazed with the realistic way that your car moves. This feeling comes so close to reality that you will call it almost perfect and unique compared with other good simulators you have already tried. The graphics and the sound is top quality too and you will enjoy driving your super car on real European racetracks (more than 11). This is a title you should not miss if you like this category of games. P.S. I give this game 4 and not five stars because of the very high requirements it demands like i.e. 3d graphic card with 32mb as minimum.
