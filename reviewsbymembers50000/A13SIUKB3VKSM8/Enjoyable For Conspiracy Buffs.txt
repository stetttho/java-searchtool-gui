 With the popularity of books like Foucault's Pendulum and DaVinci Code, books such as the Second Messiah will surely enjoy a surgence in popularity.  This treatment of Masonic history and the Shroud of Turin is interesting, well-written and many times fantastical.  I read this book many years ago and the detailed architectural descriptions of Rosslyn (or Roslin) were the most enjoyable for me.
