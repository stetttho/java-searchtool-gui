 The cover is all wrong for this book.  The heroine, Cristine, is a plus-size redhead while the hero, Gab, is Hispanic.  Yet, by the cover you would have not thought that.  This book was HOT!You come to care for both characters in a short time.   For anyone who is tired of reading books about skinny women who get their man and want to read a story about a plump and beautiful woman get her man, this is for you.
