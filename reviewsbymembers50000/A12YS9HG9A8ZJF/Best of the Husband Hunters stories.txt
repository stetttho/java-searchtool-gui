 Maxie must marry if she wants to inherit the 1 million dollars left to her by her aunt. Angelos Petronides has waited 3 years to have Maxie as his mistress. But Angelos soon finds out that marriage is the only way that  Maxie will ever be his. Of all three of the husband hunters books this was  the best one, even though as usual I felt like slapping the hero several  times for his outdated thinking.
