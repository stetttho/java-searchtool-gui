 Given the fact that the subject matter should have been fascinating and that Alan Rickman was the star, why was I disappointed in this movie?  I'll tell you why--because the events in the film were confusing enough without  a barely audible dialogue.  By the end of the movie I wondered why I sat  through the whole thing.  Go ahead and rent this at your local video store  if you don't have anything else to do, but don't buy it.
