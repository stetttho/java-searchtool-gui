 Journey to the Center of the Earth with Professor Lindenbrook (James Mason) and his geology student (Pat Boone).  The classic beauty of Arlene Dahl has been added as the love interest.  Stunningly beautiful scenes of  the underearth realm are filled with variety.  There are scenes such as  Lindenbrook and his student companion finding themselves in an eiderdown  storehouse that add a touch of comedy. James Mason's dynamic personality  really shines through in his magnificent portrayal of Professor  Lindenbrook.  This is an adventure to be enjoyed again and again.
