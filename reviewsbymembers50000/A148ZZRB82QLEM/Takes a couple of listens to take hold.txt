 At first, Aslyn sounds like she's just trying to fit in with the current pop crowd. But the more you listen, the more her individuality comes out. She is not as bubblegum pop as Ashlee Simpson, Lindsay Lohan, and Hilary Duff - and she's not as rocky as Avril Lavigne. Aslyn needs more exposure - this, along with Anna Nalick's Wreck Of The Day, has been one of the better pop releases of 2005.
