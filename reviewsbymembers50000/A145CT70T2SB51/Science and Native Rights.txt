 While I am all for science, I've always had a slight problem with the Berring Straight theory. How could a small band of hunter gatherers spread across all of the American supercontinent (Greenland, North America, the Carribean, Central America and South America) and whipe out all the megafauna within such a short time period? It seems that Vine Deloria jr. has given us his interpertation with this book. While not the best book in the world, it is a very well thought out arguement. Check it out.
