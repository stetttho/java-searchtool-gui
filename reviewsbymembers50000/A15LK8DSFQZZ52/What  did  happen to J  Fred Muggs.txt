 "The Today Show" started in 1952 as a gamble to fill morning television hours.  The show got horrible reviews and yet it's still on television 51 years later. How in the world did that happen? This book details the beginning of "The Today Show", starting with the first host, Dave Garroway. The man who is considered the father of the show, Pat Weaver is profiled here. All the information about how the show was put together, the various hosts of the first twenty-five years and all the growing pains of a long running television news show are detailed in this book. If you want to see what "The Today Show" was like in the beginning, this is the book for you.
