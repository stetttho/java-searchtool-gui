 Sure, you can dress from the Gap or Old Navy or Lane Bryant or Macy's - but is that _really_you? Probably not, since you're reading this review. This book details many wonderful things to make - for you parents who get hit with "I need a costume for _tomorrow_" at 8pm, there's a quick Native American Brave shirt made from a pillowcase - or maybe your little angel needs to be an angel for the school winter play - there's directions for that in here. too. And you don't need to be a seamtress, this starts with ready made items. there's even a beautiful crocheted jacket - you start with premade table runners. Ff you want to make a fashion statement different from anybody else, or want to help your kids break out of the rigid dictates of the "fashion police", give this book a look - you'll enjoy it.
