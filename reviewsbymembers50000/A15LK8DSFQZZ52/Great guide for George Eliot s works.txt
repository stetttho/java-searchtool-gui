 This book is not a careful analysis of all of George Eliot's works - you'd need a much bigger book to contain all of that. This book analyzes George Eliot's style of writing and the way she used the language to paint her fiction. M's Mann uses many examples from M's Eliot's art to point out the wonderful wordplay inherent in the work. M's Eliot's sarcastic remarks, puns and metaphors are carefully chosen and explained. M's Mann's love of her subject material comes shining through her book. If you are interested in George Eliot's works or you're a writer, wondering how does another writer generate plots and storylines, this is a great book for you.You will end up with a greater understanding of not only M's Eliot's work but the process of writing.
