 "The Operator" provides an in-depth view of the modern entertainment industry and its workings. Time and again, you see how the money is more important than the art. Geffen has a finger in every pie  going - music, movies, Broadway, etc. but, as the song goes, can't get no  satisfaction. He seems to be powered by an overriding need to make his  mark, name and fortune at any cost (often doing down people who've helped  him get where he is) but it's never enough. Fascinating stuff but  definitely not the kind of life I'd want to live.
