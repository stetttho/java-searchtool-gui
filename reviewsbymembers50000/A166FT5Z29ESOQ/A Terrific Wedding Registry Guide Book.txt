 Are you newly engaged and thinking about store hopping registering for all those shower and wedding gifts? Don't go without first brushing up on the subject with wedding author and expert Sharon Naylor. She writes her lastest publication, The Ultimate Wedding Registry Workbook, with updated insight into the new trends in bridal registries. Smart shopping knowledge is broken down into six parts. Convenient checklists will have you organized and spying gifts you might never have thought about. Lastly, but not least, Sharon stresses the importance of saying "Thank-You" and has wonderful advice for couples to share this task together. This book takes a fresh look at making the most out of your wedding registry. A terrific guide book.
