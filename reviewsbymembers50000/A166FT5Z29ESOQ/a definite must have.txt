 Bridal Bouquets is a beautifully photographed source book of ideas and styles that are guaranteed to help you pick the flowers that are just perfect for your wedding. You can also see which flowers will be in season at the time of your wedding. There are three main parts to this book: No. 1 - Choosing your Flowers: Types of Wedding Themes, Working with Color, Working with the Seasons and Florist. No. 2 - Flowers are organized by color themes - making it very easy to find what you are looking for. The hard part will be making the decision once you see how many choices you have. No. 3 - The Secret of Successful Color Combinations to create romantic, jeweled, or dramatic effects. This book is a definite must have when deciding on your wedding flowers and when you meet with your potential florists.
