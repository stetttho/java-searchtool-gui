 The Frugal Fish tells what fish to buy and how to store them. And it's crammed with recipes for fish tha are baked, barbecued, broiled, deep-fried, poached, sauteed, pan-fried or steamed. Plus, it has special recipes for canned fish, salted and smoked fish, and clams and mussels. There are recipes for sauces, too. And it may have more recipes for salt cod than any other cookbook.
