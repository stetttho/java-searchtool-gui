 In A Sunburned Country makes it seems as if Australia has more things that will bite or sting you, with fatal results, than any other place on earth. And, although Bill Bryson doesn't mention it, Australia even has a toad that will make you crazy if you lick it.  But you'd have to be crazy in the first place, to lick a toad,  wouldn't you? You don't have to be crazy to buy In A Sunburned Country, though. It's as funny as his other stuff. Maybe even funnier.
