 This book promises to be very durable. It has flexible plastic covers and a binding which seems to be sewn, instead of being glued together.  It's size makes it convenient for carrying on a trip, since it will easily fit in a purse, jacket pocket or fanny pack. Despite its small size, it has over 55,000 references in 672 pages. There's even an ABC Thumb Index at the edge of each page, making it easy to find any entry.
