 As an adult who enjoys anime, I enjoyed this second installment of the Master of Mosquiton series.  However, as a mom, I would not recommend this tape for younger kids due to nudity and sexual situations.  My 12 year old  child and I had watched the first tape, part one, and it had been suitable  for her to watch, so I assumed this tape, which is the second in the series  would be as well.  I assumed wrong, and I had some explaining to do after  watching this tape, let me tell you!  I've also seen part three, the tape  after this one, and it was fine for younger kids, the sexual situations and  nudity seem to be solely on this tape.
