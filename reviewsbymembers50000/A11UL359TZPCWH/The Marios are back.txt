 The Marios, Princess and Toad are back in this animated 30 minute film from the 80's.  The Bros, Princes and Toad think they are going to attend a benefit Spaghetti Dinner at the Linguini Empire Colosseum to raise money for orphan mushrooms, but it all turns out to be a plot by Evil Emperor "Augustus Septemberus Octoberus" Koopa to feed the Marios to the lions.
