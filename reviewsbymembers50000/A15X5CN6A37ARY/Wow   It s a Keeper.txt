 If you're a historical western romance lover, this is the book to read!  The three stories are great and the characters steal your heart!   Be sure not to miss the continuing story of Kincaid brother Brock in Gunslinger's Bride.  You'll also enjoy Big Sky Brides!  Two contemporary stories of sisters Suzanna and Diana Brennan and the historical western story of great-great grandparents, Isabelle and Kyle Running Horse Brennan.
