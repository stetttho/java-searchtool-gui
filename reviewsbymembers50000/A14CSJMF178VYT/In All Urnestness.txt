 A detailed and history-filled trip into the crematory, Fred Rosen sprinkles the topic with humor as a bonus for coming out the other side.    The reader learns that JFK, Jr wasn't so much "spread" as he was plopped into the Atlantic, along with other tips and minutia to dust off and impress the folks with.      A how-to, why-to and yes, you could wear a tutu guide for taking an eternal powder.  --Laurel825
