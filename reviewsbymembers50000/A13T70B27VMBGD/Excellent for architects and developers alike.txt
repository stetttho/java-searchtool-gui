 This book is a must if your are in the J2EE industry and unless you are content to being a head-down coder! All the architects and serious developers, who not only wants to make the sw work but wants to make it efficient and well-structured too, should go thru it, the examples are very helpful and categorizing the patterns in three different tiers provide a intuitive way to look at those. Without making this review any longer, what I'll suggest, NOT as a prerequisite, but as a supplement, is the Gang of Four's (Eric gamma et. al.) 'Design Patterns' book...
