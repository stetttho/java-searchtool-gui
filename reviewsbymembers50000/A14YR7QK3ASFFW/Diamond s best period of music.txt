 This is the classic period of Neil Diamond. Such AM radio classics like "Cherry Cherry," "Solitary Man," "Thank The Lord For The Nighttime." All three of these and the rest of the songs on  this disc shows why Diamond is considered one of the best songwriters of  his era. After this, he decided to get serious and never really sounded the  same After this, try to find some of his albums on the Bang label. They  will make you take a second look at this all important artist.
