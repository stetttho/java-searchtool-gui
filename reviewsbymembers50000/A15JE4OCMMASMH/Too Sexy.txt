 Although Miles' touch is evident on this one, this is all Cannonball's show. His playing is like watching a butterfly, his sound is one of the sexiest ever, and he's at his peak here on alto saxophone. Given the way Bird smashed all future dreams of anyone doing anything with the alto, Cannonball carved his niche and blew his own distintive soulful sound. This one highlights his style more than any other release as a solo artist.
