 Dr. Burns has been criticzied for being shallow, and preaching quick-fixes to emotional problems that aren't real.  This is not true.  For those that understand that no one has a cure-all method to mental health Dr. Burns  writings offer some good principles that help people learn how they  perceive the world and how that effects their mental state.  These  principles do work, I have used them and found them to be good tools for  working through my problems with other people.
