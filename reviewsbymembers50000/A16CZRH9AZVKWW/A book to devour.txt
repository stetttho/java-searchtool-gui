 In order to enjoy this book, you must be a Garcia Marquez fan or at least have read a few of his other books before.  If you pick this book up and are exposed to GGM for the first time though this, his memoirs, you will be bored to death, let alone clueless about what he is writing about. For us GGM addicts, this book is another stroke of GGM's genius.  You'll learn about the though process of the genius who practically invented magic realism.
