 Jaid Black just keeps getting better and better. If you love hot and gorgeous alpha males and strong independent heroines then you have got to read Jaid Black's work. Not for the timid, only the adventurous and daring need apply! You'll love how Ms. Black delves into a woman's secret fantasies and brings them to life on the page. One of the most talented writers around, you'll be amazed how you could have missed her. I can't wait to read more of her work.
