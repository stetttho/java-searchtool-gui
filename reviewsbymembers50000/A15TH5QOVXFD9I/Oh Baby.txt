 Jaid Black is amazing. She has created a world unlike any you've ever read before. This is the type of universe lesser authors strive forever to achieve within their pages. With every Trek Mi Q'an story her universe grows and becomes more real. Her alpha males are the best ever written and her spunky heroines give their mates what-for! Make sure you have a free evening before you start reading this because once you pick it up you won't be able to put it down. These aren't your mother's tame romances either so be prepared for some amazingly hot sex-scenes and zany adventures galore! With talent like this I'm sure we'll see this rising star become a household name in a few years. I just can't wait for more!
