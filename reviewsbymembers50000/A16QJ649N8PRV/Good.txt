 'Stronger' by Natalie Grant is a very enjoyable album.  Natalie has a beautiful voice that can create some really amazing sounds.  Her best known songs on this album are 'Keep on Shining' and 'What Other Man'.  These really highlight the talent that she has.  There are several other good songs on this album, as well.  Although she may not be as well known as some other artists, Natalie has a great sound and the ability to do really great things with it.
