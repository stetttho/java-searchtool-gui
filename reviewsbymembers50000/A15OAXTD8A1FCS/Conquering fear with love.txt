 Onions, Onions is a story of love conquering fear. A young married woman is afraid of evil spirits and believes that onions will keep them at bay. So she serves onions at every meal. When her husband protests and tries to make light of her fears, she starts hiding her compulsion. It is only when he leaves her that they both realize that their love is true and stronger than their obsessions. Do they live happily ever after? Well, they ARE in love.
