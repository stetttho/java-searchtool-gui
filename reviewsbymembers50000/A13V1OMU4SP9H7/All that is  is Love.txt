 Tony has Nothing to say and he says it beautifully!  After reading this little book several times, the "i" that appeared to be reading began to blessedly, fade.   It is glimpsed in this apparent person that All that Is, Is Love, is Freedom, is No Thing.  Tony answered questions that the mind was unaware of, leaving a sense of space and natural relaxation ...   Give it a shot. There is everything to lose and Nothing to gain! And in that Nothing, there IS Love. That is all.
