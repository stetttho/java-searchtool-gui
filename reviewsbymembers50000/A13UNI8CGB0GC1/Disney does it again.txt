 Let's face it, Disney is the big name it is for a reason.  That reason being - you aren't let down.  Sure, you've seen Snow White, but this version brings DVD to a whole new level.  The extras are interesting, entertaining, and creatively produced.  The magic of Disney continues on, never breaking the illusion while offering up secrets of the production.  As usual, Disney takes to mind it's whole audience by giving you extras that will entertain at least one member of your family at any given time.  And the Barbra Streisand version of Someday My Prince Will Come is stirring, to say the least.  For fans of Snow White, or fans of Disney in general, you will not be disappointed!
