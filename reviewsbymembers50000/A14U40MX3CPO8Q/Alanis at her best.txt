 Alanis' first cd, "Jagged Little Pill", was definately a hard act to follow.  However, with her latest cd, Alanis Morissette proves she's here to stay.  Her words are beautiful and it's as though she's talking to  the listener.  Alanis has a tremendously beautiful and expressive voice,  and "Supposed Former Infatuation Junkie" proves it.  For a deep  and introspective album, purchase Alanis Morissette's latest release now.
