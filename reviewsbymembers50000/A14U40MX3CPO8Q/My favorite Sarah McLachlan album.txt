 Since her debut in 1988, Sarah McLachlan has grown into becoming one of the most beloved singer/songwriters of our time.  Whether you're listening to the catchy "Building A Mystery", or the sad and profound  "Full of Grace", this album has it all.  Sarah is a great  songwriter who is willing to take chances, all with one heck of a beautiful  voice.  For personal and relaxing music that will take you on a journey,  pick up "Surfacing" or any of Sarah McLachlan's albums.
