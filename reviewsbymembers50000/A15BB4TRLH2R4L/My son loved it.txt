 This was an unusually easy Hotwheels item to use and assemble. You have to wait 15 minutes for the plastic to melt and then 30 minutes for it to cool (so you have to be patient). My only complaint is that you're supposed to leave it assembled between uses, so of course it doesn't fit back into the box. In the end, we got really cool cars that we were able to detail with decals. ...
