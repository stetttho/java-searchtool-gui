 The great brands have authentic stories behind them, and the best advertising still is the truth well-told.  Nike's irreverence, devotion to athletes, and disdain for compromised athletic shoes isn't a fiction; it's the essence of the company and the conviction of every employee.  Apple's story is not a fiction, a ruse, or an artifice; it's the essence of the company and the passion of its employees.  This list goes on: Harley-Davidson, BodyWorks, Nordstrom, Target.    The author assume that everyone is fooling everyone because he does, and gets away with it.  But life is long.  Does his brand endure?  Let's watch; many of us suspect not.
