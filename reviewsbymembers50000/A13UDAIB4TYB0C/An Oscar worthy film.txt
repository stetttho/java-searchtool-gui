 The Lord of the Rings: Fellowship of the Ring has raised the bar for fantasy films. Tolkien wrote the book of the century and Jackson captured the spirit and the essence of Tolkien beautifully. I agree the effects were wonderful but didn't come across as playng with digital toys just for the sake of effects. It would have been impossible to capture all of the elements of the story and kept the film under 6 hours. But Jackson captured the spirit of Tolkien and gave newbies a reason to dive into the books. I'm counting the days to the next film The Two Towers. ...
