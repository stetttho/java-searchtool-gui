 It was intersting to read a book on Kiss that told their incredible story from the perspective of people who worked with the band rather then in the band; people that had a professional and financial interest.  C.K. Lendt provided management services for Kiss during much of the supergroup's heyday and takes readers deep into some very interesting affairs that will shock and possibly revolt some readers. "Kiss And Sell" is not some cheesy gossip laden trash where the author is trying to cash in on knowing a few celebrities. No this book is far from that. Here we learn about the biggest band in the land, straight from a man who was largely responsible for their growth; a man who was instrumental in marketing them.  Sales people would do well to read this. Marketing and sales go hand in hand. Not to many products are marketed as well as Kiss was. Here's how it was done.
