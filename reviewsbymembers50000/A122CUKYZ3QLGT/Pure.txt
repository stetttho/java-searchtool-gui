 Of course no album is perfect, but the purity with which Mr. Newman's music comes through in these interpretations is riveting. "I Think It's Going to Rain Today" alone is worth the price of the album. As William Merrill wrote in his spotlight review, the songwriting takes center stage. If, like me, you've not been to a Randy Newman solo concert, at least we've got this album to bring that songwriting alive for us.
