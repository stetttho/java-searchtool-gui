 Just when you thought it was the final voyage of the original Enterprise crew , and Captain Kirk, you were wrong!!! Not only does this book show some fascinating insight from Shatner in regards to Captain Kirk's persona, it also brings the original Enterprise crew together to help defeat Kirk's enemy Androvar Drake! The novel also deals with Kirk's battle with the aging process, and how he feels his time will finally be over that is until he meets the seductive Teilani who thanks to some good writing from Shatner is indeed very beautiful, and the woman to die for! But there is still more to come from the rest of Shatner's novels! A must for die hard trekkies or Kirk fans!
