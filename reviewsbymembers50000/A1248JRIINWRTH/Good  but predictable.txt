 AFTER TWILIGHT is a contemporary romantic suspense that you just can't put down. The mystery in the story is predictable, but the book is so fast-paced and well-written that it just doesn't matter. The relationship between Braedon and Kacy developes at high speed. Man, wouldn't it be nice if you could just fall in love and forgive everything and look forward to tomorrow? That's what happens in AFTER TWILIGHT.
