 Rosalind Carmichael being the devoted sister to her get in trouble brother Tony has to borrow money from someone she hates the most.  Sam Winter has always had feeling for Rosalind, and is determined to get her to be a permanent part of his life.  Rosalind puts her engagment to her stuffy fiance to the test by agreeing to "live" with Sam to repay a debt.  What she does not count on is seeing Sam for the truly loving, generous man who is actually better for her.  The question is will she make the right choice or will Sam lose her forever?
