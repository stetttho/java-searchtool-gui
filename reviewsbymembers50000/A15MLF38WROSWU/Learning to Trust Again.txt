 Professor Madison Evans goes on sabbatical on the Virginia West Coast.  She meets handsome antiques dealer Antonio Gates and ends up under his spell.  Tony is resistant to romance, but he is smitten with Madison.  Can they both overcome mistrust, betrayal to love once again?  Very enjoyable book.  Sensual, comical and had that one character you loved to hate.  Mama Lou was may favorite.  Excellent read and a nice ending.
