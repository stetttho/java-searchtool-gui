 After a nasty divorce Emma Jones feels she is happy with nothing but her work.  Just like most of us have, there is that one friend that won't stop meddling with our love life and always finds a way to try and set you up with the eligible bachelor.  That bachelor is Jordan Everett.  Jordan somehow makes Emma realize she did in fact miss being in a relationship and she falls in love.  Then someone from Jordan's past manages to manipulates him and......I will let you read the rest.   It's a good story of learning to love again and not allowing someone to try and manipulate you in life.  It's a story of taking chances.
