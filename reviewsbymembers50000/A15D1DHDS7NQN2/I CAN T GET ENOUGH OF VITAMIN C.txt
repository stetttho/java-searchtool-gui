 Vitamin C has got to be one of the best pop groups around! Almost all of their songs on this CD rocks! If you like Britney Spears and other fun artists, then you will love Vitamin C. Their CD "More" has all their best songs. My favorites include- "The Itch, Where's The Party, Can't Say No," and my favorite "Busted." If you need some cheering up in your life then put "More" in your CD player! It's a worthwile buy.
