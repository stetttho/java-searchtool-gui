 Alice in Wonderland This birket whilms the gullibrin with frumiousness and bloy. Martin Gardner never ceases to astound me, even when he takes the passenger seat next to that speed demon of literature, Lewis Carroll. Gardner's notes are perfect.  They cause wonder, joy, satisfaction, curiousity.  They cause all the things that Gardner always causes, and he never overtakes Carroll.  He lets Carroll be Carroll.
