 Michigan Central University has a serial killer amongst it's student body.  But what would drive an ordinary college student in becoming what the media has labeled "The Mid-West Strangler"?  Could one lie coupled with a childhood secret change a regular college student into a heartless killer? Detective Madeline Harris is sent undercover as a college student to help catch the killer.  But even being one of Detroit's most decorated African American detectives may not be enough to help Madeline catch The Mid West Strangler.    Author Derek Vitatoe expertly narrates this story in the voices of the serial killer and the detective assigned to bring him down.  Just when readers think that the story has reached it's dramatic conclusion Mr. Vitatoe delivers a stunner that will leave readers mouths hanging open and begging for a part II.
