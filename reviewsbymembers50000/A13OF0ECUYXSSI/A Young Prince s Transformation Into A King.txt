 Hood Rich is a tear-jerking story chronicling the life of DeMarqeus Prince.  At the on-set of the book Prince is being interviewed from behind bars for a magazine.   It is from this point where readers will get to know young Prince and the life struggles he had to face.  Fatherhood, gunplay, murder and eventually prison is all experienced before he turns 18.  Prince is a true product of his environment and unfortunately pays the price for it.  The hardships faced by Prince will cause you to shed many tears.  At so many points in the book I questioned just how much would this boy be forced to endure?  Although the process was long and turbulent young Prince eventually transforms into a king and readers will definitely enjoy the journey.  Hood Rich is an excellent story that will touch your soul.
