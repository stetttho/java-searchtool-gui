 Ms. Williams does a wonderfull job of weaving an intricate tale of a family in crisis.  After being released from prison Loretta is faced with the difficult task of attempting to raise the two children she left behind.    But when Loretta becomes more interested in re-claiming her lost youth than helping her children through their personal crisises all types of trouble ensue.  Especially with her son Nasir who has serious baby Momma Drama.  Driven is an enjoyeable read which will leave the reader eagerly anticipating the sequel "At The Court's Mercy".
