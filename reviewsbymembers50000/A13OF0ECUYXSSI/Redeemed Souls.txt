 Richard Jeanty tells a powerful story in Neglected Souls, chronicling the lives of Nina and Jimmy who were raised in the slums by their drug addicted, prostituting mother Katrina.  Katrina's downward spiral leads her to commit suicide leaving her two teenage children to fend for themselves.  Forced with such a harsh reality Nina and Jimmy somehow manage to pull together and pull themselves out of poverty.    Readers will root for Nina and Jimmy while they overcome incredible odds to become successful adults.  Kudos to Mr. Jeanty for writing an urban novel that depicts what can be achieved through hard work and perseverance.
