 Anna J will make your temperature rise with this one.  Inviting a third party in your bedroom takes a turn for the worse in this erotic thriller.  James and Jasmine Cinque have no idea what they're in for after deciding to spice up their marriage with a little menage-a-trois.    I don't want to give anything away but this book will have you speaking out loud like, "Oh Hell No!!!"  I would love to see this book on the big screen but it would definatley get a NC-17 rating. If you like erotic suspense it don't get much better than this.  Anna J is gonna make Zane step her game up because there is a definitely new player on the field.
