 This is a very, very, very interesting book about what really went on in classic Hollywood. It is filled with fascinating stories about Jean Harlow, Carole Landis, Marion Davies, Clark Gable, Judy Garland, Joan Crawford, Lana Turner, and just about every other classic star. This book exposes the truth behind some of Hollywood's most famous scandals. It's filled with juicy gossip and some rare photos. Once you start reading this book it's impossible to put down!!!
