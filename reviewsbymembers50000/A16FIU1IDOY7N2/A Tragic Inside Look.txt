 This is a compelling read.  The Dynamite Kid and the British Bulldog were on top of the wrestling industry many years ago.  Tom Billington pulls no punches (pardon the pun) in this account of his rise to superstardom and the price he paid.  If your idea about wrestling is that it is just a fake show and nobody gets hurt, you need to read this.  He talks about the drugs, the egos, and the management decisions that can make or ruin a career.  Check it out!
