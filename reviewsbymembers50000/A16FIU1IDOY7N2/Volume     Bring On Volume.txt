 It is so obvious the respect that Vestal Goodman has earned.  Just look at the line up of singers who are considered Vestal's "Friends" and listen to each of them sing with this great lady of song.  Vestal's voice reaches out and grabs the listener with the passion of the lyrics and the deep conviction that she sings with.  If Vestal's name is on a recording, you can be sure that you won't be disappointed.
