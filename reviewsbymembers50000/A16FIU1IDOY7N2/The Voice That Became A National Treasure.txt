 When DJ's originally heard that Elvis Presley was going to record gospel music, the industry was afraid that no one would accept this "rockabilly" singer singing sacred.  The record company was very reluctant to release the Elvis Gospel songs.  When they finally came out, these became some of the best selling releases of Elvis's career.  That voice will linger in your mind and heart long after you turn off the tape/CD player. The messages of the songs a very much like the man who sings them: Timeless!
